Project Overview

This investment thesis provides a detailed analysis of Tips Music, one of India's leading music companies, and the broader Indian music industry. The study evaluates market trends, competitive dynamics, and growth opportunities in the digital music space.

Professional Context

Conducted during my tenure at YourNest Capital, this project was part of our firm's initiative to explore opportunities in India's growing digital entertainment sector. The analysis contributed to our firm's understanding of the music industry's transformation and potential investment opportunities.

Research Methodology

  • Industry analysis and market sizing
  • Competitor benchmarking
  • Financial statement analysis
  • Consumer behavior research
  • Technology trend assessment
  • Regulatory environment review

Key Insights

The analysis uncovered several important trends and opportunities:

  • Rapid growth in digital music consumption
  • Increasing smartphone penetration driving music streaming
  • Strong regional music market potential
  • Emerging monetization models in the industry
  • Strategic partnerships and licensing opportunities

Streaming Revenue Analysis

Calculation of streaming revenue and growth metrics

Python 
def calculate_streaming_metrics(streams, price_per_stream, growth_rate):
    # Calculate monthly revenue
    monthly_revenue = streams * price_per_stream
    
    # Project future streams
    projected_streams = []
    current_streams = streams
    
    for month in range(12):
        current_streams *= (1 + growth_rate)
        projected_streams.append(current_streams)
    
    # Calculate projected revenue
    projected_revenue = [s * price_per_stream for s in projected_streams]
    
    return {
        'current_revenue': monthly_revenue,
        'projected_streams': projected_streams,
        'projected_revenue': projected_revenue,
        'total_projected_revenue': sum(projected_revenue)
    }

Market Share Analysis

Analysis of market share and competitive position

Python 
def analyze_market_share(company_data, market_data):
    # Calculate market share
    market_share = company_data['revenue'] / market_data['total_revenue']
    
    # Calculate growth relative to market
    market_growth = market_data['growth_rate']
    company_growth = company_data['growth_rate']
    relative_growth = company_growth / market_growth
    
    # Calculate competitive position
    position = {
        'market_share': market_share,
        'relative_growth': relative_growth,
        'market_rank': len([x for x in market_data['competitors'] 
                          if x['revenue'] > company_data['revenue']]) + 1
    }
    
    return position

ROI Calculation

Investment return analysis with multiple scenarios

Python 
def calculate_roi(investment, cash_flows, discount_rate):
    # Calculate NPV
    npv = sum(cf / (1 + discount_rate) ** i 
             for i, cf in enumerate(cash_flows))
    
    # Calculate ROI
    roi = (npv - investment) / investment
    
    # Calculate payback period
    cumulative_cf = np.cumsum(cash_flows)
    payback = np.argmax(cumulative_cf >= investment)
    
    return {
        'npv': npv,
        'roi': roi,
        'payback_period': payback,
        'irr': np.irr([-investment] + cash_flows)
    }
